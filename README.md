# JalBindu – Water Footprint Calculator (PSCS_4)  
**Smart India Hackathon 2025 | Ministry of Jal Shakti**

### Problem Statement (Official – PSCS_4)
**Title:** Use of Digital Technology to calculate water footprints for different daily use items.

### Official Description (Ministry of Jal Shakti)
A user-friendly mobile app / website that instantly shows the water footprint (in litres) of everyday items by:
- Scanning the product using the camera (like Google Lens), OR
- Simple search / text input  
Must support multiple Indian languages to enable pan-India usage and create mass awareness about hidden water consumption in daily life.

### Goal
Make the invisible water footprint of daily products visible to every Indian citizen, promote mindful consumption, and contribute to water conservation and drought prevention.

### SDG Alignment
SDG 3 (Good Health), SDG 6 (Clean Water & Sanitation), SDG 9 (Innovation), SDG 13 (Climate Action)

### Features Implemented
- Camera-based product scanning & water footprint display
- Manual search with 1000+ daily Indian products
- Multilingual support (English + Hindi + regional languages)
- Beautiful visualizations & awareness tips
- Offline-capable database

### Tech Stack
Flutter • Firebase / Supabase • Google ML Kit • Open-source Water Footprint Dataset

100% software solution | No hardware required | Built in <36 hours for SIH 2025
